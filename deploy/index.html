ABOUT US

Clover Event Co. is a boutique wedding planning company specializing in planning, styling, and coordinating authentic weddings for modern couples. Started in 2015 by Owner Caitlin Maloney Kuchemba, Clover has grown and expanded into a beautiful team of wedding coordinators. We now service throughout the Philadelphia area.
